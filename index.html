Why Visit Coventry?

Located in the heart of England, Coventry is a city that blends history and modern innovation. From its stunning cathedral ruins to the impressive cultural events as a former UK City of Culture, Coventry offers visitors a unique experience.

Explore its medieval streets, learn about British transport heritage, and enjoy the lively student and creative communities that give the city its friendly energy
